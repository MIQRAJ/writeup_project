The difference between paddings, margins, and borders

Borders: Border is The perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line covering the content

Padding: Padding is The inner space between the content and the border of your box.

Margin: Margin is The outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
